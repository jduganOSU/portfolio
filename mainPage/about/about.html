Personal Introduction

Crafting solutions through code, one line at a time. Hey there, I'm Jack Dugan! Currently immersing myself in the dynamic world of computer science, my journey here is a blend of passion and purpose. I originally graduated from Moravian University in 2018 with a Finance degree and spent four enriching years as an investment analyst/associate. While in college, I had my first taste of coding, and though it was brief, it left an impression.

Fast forward to 2022, when a personal project nudged me to dive deep into the realm of software and web development. Tasked with gathering historical NBA data, I turned to Python, guided by Harvard's CS50 course, and engineered a web scraping program. The exhilaration of turning an idea into a functional piece of software was transformative. It wasn't just the code; it was the ability to craft solutions and see immediate results that captivated me. Recognizing this newfound passion was not fleeting, I committed to formalizing my education, enrolling in Oregon State University's post-baccalaureate Computer Science program. As I continue to navigate and learn, I'm eager to harness the power of technology to create impactful solutions.

Professional Journey

After securing my degree from Moravian University, I embarked on a rewarding journey with an investment firm, specializing in its real estate assets. The intricate nature of real estate investment honed my attention to detail, while the need to convey information sharpened my communication skills.

These competencies, I believe, are universally valuable. Whether it's poring over lines of code to ensure functionality, or translating tech jargon into clear, comprehensible insights for a team, the discipline and clarity I've cultivated will enhance my contributions in the tech realm and beyond.

Future Goals

As I look towards the future, my ambition is to immerse myself in cutting-edge technological domains that captivate me. I'm fundamentally drawn to the challenge of understanding complex systems and harnessing the latest in technology to craft innovative solutions. It's not just about understanding the current landscape but also about anticipating what's next and positioning myself to be an active contributor in that evolution. I seek environments that encourage continuous learning and collaboration, where I can both contribute and grow, translating my passions into tangible solutions and experiences.
