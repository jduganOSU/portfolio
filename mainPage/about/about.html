Personal Introduction

Hey everyone! My name is Jack Dugan, a current computer science student with a thirst for learning. I first graduated from Moravian University in 2018 with a degree in Finance. I have spent the last 4 years working as an investment analyst/associate at an investment firm. My journey towards software/web development started in 2022 when I embarked on a project that required gathering historical NBA data. To do so, I taught myself Python by taking Harvard's CS50 online course, then built a simple web scraping program.

This self-initiated challenge not only re-introduced me to coding (I dabbled in college) but ignited a curiosity to understand and craft technology solutions. I soon realized that the joy I derived from coding wasn't just a passing phase but a genuine passion. Consequently, I enrolled in the post-baccalaurette Computer Science program at Oregon State University.

Professional Journey

After graduating from Moravian University, I started a job in an investment firm, where I have been for the past 4 years. My role has mostly centered around the real estate assets of the firm, an asset group I have always found interesting. These last 4 years have taught me alot, especially the importance of communication and attention to detail. The skills I have learned over the last few years will certainly translate well both in my future career and personal life.

Future Goals

I aspire to be working on tech that is exciting and beneficial to society.
